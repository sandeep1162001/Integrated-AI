Artificial intelligence (AI) can help retailers in many ways, including:

  1. Customer experience

    AI enhances customer service through personalized interactions using chatbots and in-store kiosks. It also optimizes in-store displays based on sales data analysis.

  2. Marketing

    AI maximizes marketing campaign effectiveness by analyzing customer interactions, optimizing pricing strategies, and using computer vision for targeted promotions.

  3. Inventory management

    AI streamlines inventory management, reduces shrinkage, and enhances decision-making efficiency for store associates.

  4. Demand forecasting and assortment planning

    AI helps retailers forecast demand accurately and plan assortments to meet customer preferences, improving inventory turnover and customer satisfaction.

  5. Loss prevention

    AI assists in preventing losses through advanced analytics and monitoring, minimizing risks related to theft and operational errors.

  6. Other applications

    AI can generate product descriptions and marketing content using generative AI, enable visual search for customers, and innovate various retail processes.
